Reliable, cost-effective communications for races, parades, and public events

Why Ham Radio? Key Benefits Getting Started

Why Use Ham Radio?

Independent & Reliable

Voice and data mesh network that works even when cellular or Wi‑Fi is overloaded or down. Off‑grid power via battery, solar, or generator keeps communications alive for hours.

Rapid Deployment

Portable repeater or mobile command station can be set up in under 30 minutes and moved as the route or event needs change.

Emergency Services Link

Trained operators coordinate directly with police, fire, EMS, and local officials. Real‑time logging records every call, incident, and resource movement for post‑event analysis.

Key Benefits

Cost‑Effective

Volunteer clubs provide commercial‑grade reliability at a fraction of the price of equipment rentals.

Safety & Liability

Faster incident reporting shortens response times, reducing injuries and insurance costs.

Scalable Coverage

From a single handheld pair for a small parade to a multi‑repeater network for city‑wide marathons.

Community Goodwill

Partnering with local ham clubs demonstrates civic engagement and can boost sponsor perception.

Technical Synergy

RF expertise complements your networking and security architecture. Data can feed directly into command‑center dashboards.

After‑Action Review

Built‑in logs provide ready‑made timeline for post‑event reporting and continuous improvement.

How to Get Started

1. Contact Your Local Club

Most amateur radio clubs have a public‑service committee ready to support community events. Browse our club directory to find clubs in your area.

2. Define Event Scope

Outline your timeline, route, expected participants, and any special hazards or challenges.

3. Secure Frequencies

Coordinate with the ham club and local authorities to secure shared frequencies for the event.

4. Integrate with ICS

Assign a liaison officer to the ham team to integrate communications into your Incident Command System.

5. Run Pre‑Event Test

Verify coverage, power, and interoperability with emergency services before event day to ensure smooth operations.
