About Jack Parks (KQ4JP)

Licensed amateur radio operator serving the Atlanta metro area. Active in public service communications, ARES/AUXCOMM operations, and community emergency preparedness.

I’m Jack Parks, a licensed amateur radio operator specializing in public‑service communications, portable operations, and community outreach. With solid technical expertise and hands‑on experience in emergency communications, I focus on ensuring safety and situational awareness during events and activations. I’m dedicated to mentoring both new and seasoned hams, organizing training sessions, and coordinating public‑service activities that strengthen local networks and improve overall readiness.

You'll often see me working out in the field along side my wife, Jaime - KQ4KZV

I’ll gladly present to your club on topics related to Public Service and Amateur Radio. I’ve previously presented at Stone Mountain Hamfest and was featured on Ham Nation in 2024, where I discussed Personal Cybersecurity.
